I am a Research Analyst I at SecureBio working on the [Nucleic Acid Observatory (NAO)](https://naobservatory.org/) where I'm enhancing our production grade computational pipeline for analyzing untargeted metagenomic seqeuncing data. I am additionally a research affiliate at MIT Media Lab where I work under [Kevin Esvelt](https://www.media.mit.edu/people/esvelt/overview/) exploring blood-based biosurviellance. My interests lie in **computational biology**, **biosecurity**, and **foundation models**.

I did my undergrad in CS from UW-Madison, where I had the privilege of being mentored by [Sushmita Roy](https://roylab.discovery.wisc.edu/), [Junjie Hu](https://junjiehu.github.io/), and [Stav Atir](https://www.stavatir.com/). During my time there, I developed methods for understanding gene regulation through analyzing single cell omic datasets (scRNA-seq & scATAC-seq), with orthogonal work in transformer interpretability. I had the pleasure of starting two startups and [one student organization](https://wiscobiosec.org/) (the latter of which is still operating), all of which were cool learning experiences.
