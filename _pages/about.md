I am a research affiliate at the MIT Media Lab where I work under [Kevin Esvelt](https://www.media.mit.edu/people/esvelt/overview/). My interests are in the intersection of **computational biology** and **biosecurity**.

I did my undergrad in CS from UW-Madison, where I had the privilege of being mentored by [Sushmita Roy](https://roylab.discovery.wisc.edu/), [Junjie Hu](https://junjiehu.github.io/), and [Stav Atir](https://www.stavatir.com/). During my time there, I focused on developing methods for analyzing single cell omic datasets (scRNA-seq & scATAC-seq), with orthogonal work in mechanistic interpretability. I also had the pleasure of starting two startups, both of which failed, but were wonderful learning experiences.

If you have any questions or would like to connect, please feel free to reach out.
